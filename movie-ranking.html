The best three movies according to Sanyam(ie ME)

My top three movies of all time are:

Violet Evergarden movie

The BEST anime movie(according to me). The visuals,plot,characters(especially VIOLET!!).I love everything about this movie.

Your name

The visuals,soundtrack and the fan favourite trope of both the mcs getting separated and the reconciled. This movie is everything and contains everthing a novice would love about his first anime movie.
